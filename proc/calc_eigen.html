This subroutine calculates the relative electric and magnetic field amplitudes, the relative fluctuations in the density and velocity of all species, and the heating rates of the given solution. It is based on the calc_eigen routine by Greg Howes and Kris Klein, found in PLUME. The splitting by mechanisms is described in Huang, Howes, and Brown, JPP 2024.
